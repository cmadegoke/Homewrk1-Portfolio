About me

My name is catherine Adegoke. I am the Ceo of Yoni African Beads. Beads making has been in my generation for over thousand years . According to My Great Grandmom, it was a hobby for the women in the village till the europeans came to african and employed many women to mass produce the beads . These beads were made out of stones and Gold. In this era, we use any anything from Stones, plastics and Glass to then my elder sisters and finally to Me. I plan to teach my Daughters and their Daughters

Apart from the its being my hobby, it is my source of income. i make from 20 to 30 beads in a month.
